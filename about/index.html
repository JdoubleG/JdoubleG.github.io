About



Based in New York, NY, James Garijo-Garde is an Software Engineer in Meta's AI Infrastructure team and a Master of Computer Science candidate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. In addition to his current professional and academic endeavors, James has a personal interest in psychology and the cognitive sciences dating back to his undergraduate years at Tufts University and his work in the Center for Applied Brain & Cognitive Sciences. James is an avid technology hobbyist and tinkerer. In his spare time, James most enjoys spending time with friends but also enjoys relaxing with some good music or television.
